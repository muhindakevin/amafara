## How to Use

1. Go to **Login** page
2. You'll see a **dropdown** to select your role
3. Choose the role you want to explore
4. Click "Continue with Demo"
5. Enter any **6-digit code** (e.g., 123456) for OTP
6. Click "Verify & Login"
7. You'll be taken to that role's dashboard!

## Testing Tips

- Try each role to see different capabilities
- Notice how the sidebar menu changes per role
- Each dashboard has role-specific features
- You can switch languages (🌍 icon) to see Kinyarwanda translations
